# Covered Ram
*Large object*

**Armor Class:** 15

**Hit Points:** 100

**Damage Immunities:** poison, psychic

A  [covered ram][Item Link] consists of a movable gallery equipped with a heavy log suspended from two roof beams by chains. The log is shod in iron and used to batter through doors and barricades.

It takes a minimum of four Medium creatures to operate a ram. Because of the gallery roof, these operators have total cover against attacks from above.

**Ram.** Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one object. Hit: 16 (3d10) bludgeoning damage.
