# Suspended Cauldron
*Large object*

**Armor Class:** 19

**Hit Points:** 20

**Damage Immunities:** poison, psychic

A [cauldron][Item Link] is an Iron pot suspended so that it can be tipped easily, spilling its contents. Once emptied, a cauldron must be refilled—and its contents must usually be reheated—before it can be used again. It takes three Actions to fill a cauldron and one action to tip it.

Cauldrons can be filled with other liquids, such as acid or Green Slime, with different Effects.

**Boiling Oil.** The cauldron pours Boiling Oil onto a 10-foot-square area directly below it. Any creature in the area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
