# Cart
Carts are the cheapest and simplest of animal drawn vehicles. They consist of a cargo are above one axle.

___ 
> ## Cart
>*Large object (5 ft. by 2.5 ft.)*
>___
> - **Cost:** 15 gp
> - **Travel Speed:** 3 mph per hour (24 miles per day)
> - **Crew:** 1
> - **Passengers:** 2
> - **Weight:** 200 lbs
> - **Cargo (tons):** .575 (1,150 lbs)
